Rafting Site Plan

Sam Peterson

Overview

Purpose

Attract adventure seekers who want to go white water rafting. The site should feel dynamic and exciting to showcase our white water rafting tours.

Audience

Who are the target customers? Describe their life (or business) situation (age, financial situation, interests, and so on) The target customers are likely to be of a wide range of ages, from teenagers to retirees, but they are most likely to be young to middle-aged adults. They are likely to have a moderate to high income, as white water rafting is considered a luxury activity. Their interests may include outdoor sports and activities, adventure, travel, and nature.

What do they want? The target customers want to experience the thrill and excitement of white water rafting. They want to challenge themselves physically and mentally, and to have an adventure that they will remember for a lifetime. They are also likely to be interested in learning about the natural environment and wildlife in the area.

What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website? When accessing the website, the target customers will expect to find information on the different types of white water rafting trips that are available, as well as the level of difficulty of each trip and the associated cost. They will want to know what safety measures are in place, what equipment will be provided, and the duration of each trip. Additionally, they will want to know the minimum and maximum number of people allowed per trip, the location of the white water rafting company, the experience level of the guides, and the cancellation policy.
